üîµ BUTTON 1 ‚Äî SWITCH TO STUDY PERMIT (Become a Student)

Use this option if your Work Permit is ending and you want to stay in Canada by studying. This option is for workers changing their status to student, not only current students.

When This Option Works

  • You receive an acceptance letter from a college or university.
  • You are ready to study full-time.
  • You have funds for tuition and living costs.

Documents You Usually Need

  • Passport
  • College / University Acceptance Letter
  • Proof of Funds (tuition + living expenses)
  • Previous Education Documents
  • Short Explanation Letter (why you want to study)

Approval Chance

Medium to Good if:

  • Program matches your background or experience.
  • Funds are clear and stable.
  • School is a recognized public college or university.

If Approved

  • You legally become a Student in Canada.
  • You can study full-time.
  • You may work part-time if your permit allows.

If Refused

You may switch to Visitor status or consider leaving Canada.

Programs That Are Stronger Choices

  • Public Colleges / Universities
  • 1‚Äì2 Year Diplomas
  • Bachelor / Master / PhD Degrees

These programs often help later with:

  • Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)
  • Spouse Open Work Permit
  • Permanent Residence pathways

üîπ IMPORTANT FLEXIBILITY NOTE ‚Äî YOU CAN CHANGE PATH LATER

Starting studies does NOT lock you forever. If during your studies you find a better legal opportunity (for example, a skilled job or another valid pathway), you may switch status anytime ‚Äî as long as you follow IRCC rules and apply properly.

  • Always keep your permit valid.
  • Do not work or change activities without authorization.
  • Submit new applications before expiry dates.

Key Message: You have flexibility, but always stay within official immigration rules to keep your status safe.

üü¢ BUTTON 2 ‚Äî EXTEND OR CHANGE WORK PERMIT

Use this option if you want to continue working in Canada.

Option A ‚Äî Extend With Same Employer

Documents

  • Job Offer / Employment Letter
  • Current Work Permit Copy
  • Passport
  • Employer Compliance Number (if LMIA-exempt)

Approval Chance: Good if employer supports and documents are complete.

Result if Approved: Continue working legally.

Option B ‚Äî LMIA-EXEMPT WORK PERMIT (When Employer Cannot Provide LMIA)

1. Intra-Company Transfer (ICT)

Who: Employee of a foreign company with a Canadian branch.

Documents: Proof of 1 year foreign employment, company relationship proof, Canadian job offer.

Approval Chance: Medium to Good if companies are real and connected.

2. C11 ‚Äî Self-Employed / Business Owner

Who: Person who owns and runs a real business in Canada.

Documents: Business registration, bank statements, client contracts, invoices, tax records, simple business plan.

Approval Chance: Medium ‚Äî business must be active and genuine.

3. Spouse Open Work Permit

Who: If your spouse/partner studies or works in an eligible skilled job.

Documents: Marriage/relationship proof, partner's permit copy, job or enrollment letter, passports.

Approval Chance: Good if relationship and employment/study are genuine.

üü° BUTTON 3 ‚Äî SWITCH TO VISITOR STATUS (Temporary Safe Option)

Use this option if no work or study option is ready yet but you want to stay legally while planning.

Documents

  • Passport
  • Proof of Funds (bank statements)
  • Short Explanation Letter

Approval Chance: Usually Good if applied before expiry.

Limitations

  • Cannot work
  • Cannot study full-time
  • Temporary solution only

üîπ IMPORTANT NOTE ‚Äî VISITOR STATUS CAN HELP YOU BUY TIME

Switching to Visitor status gives you legal time to plan calmly. While on visitor status you cannot work or study full-time, but you can:

  • Search for employers who may support a future work permit
  • Prepare Study Permit applications
  • Explore PR or other legal pathways

Do not panic ‚Äî Visitor status is often used as a temporary bridge. If you later receive a job offer or school admission, you can apply for a Work Permit or Study Permit while holding valid visitor status, as long as you follow IRCC rules.

üî¥ BUTTON 4 ‚Äî VULNERABLE WORKER HELP

Use this if you are facing abuse, threats, or unsafe working conditions.

Proof Examples

  • Messages / Emails
  • Photos or recordings
  • Witness statements
  • Complaint records

Benefit: Open Work Permit that allows you to leave the abusive employer and work elsewhere.

Approval Chance: Depends on the strength of evidence, but this option exists to protect workers.

‚è∞ IF YOUR WORK PERMIT ALREADY EXPIRED ‚Äî RESTORATION

If your permit expired less than 90 days ago:

  • You may apply for Restoration of Status.
  • You cannot work until it is approved.
  • Approval Chance: Lower than applying before expiry, but still possible.

FINAL FRIENDLY REMINDER

Early planning gives you more legal choices. No option guarantees approval ‚Äî the final decision is always made by immigration officers. Always double-check information on official government sources before applying.

Stay calm. Stay legal. Plan early.
